BASIC METRICS:
-------------
1. Total Ad Spend = SUM(marketing_data[Ad_Spend])

4. RTO Orders = CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(sales), Status = "RTO")
5. Cancelled Orders = CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(sales), Status = "Cancelled")

PERFORMANCE METRICS:
-------------------
6. RTO% = (RTO Orders / Total Orders) Ã— 100
   â†’ CRITICAL: Lower is better! Target: <15%

7. Total Revenue = CALCULATE(SUM(Order_Value), Status = "Delivered")
   â†’ Only delivered orders generate revenue!

8. Lost Revenue (RTO) = CALCULATE(SUM(Order_Value + Shipping_Cost), Status = "RTO")
   â†’ This is money LOST on returns!

ACQUISITION METRICS:
-------------------
9. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = Total Ad Spend / Delivered Orders
   â†’ How much it costs to acquire ONE successful customer

10. Effective CAC = Total Ad Spend / (Total Orders - RTO Orders - Cancelled Orders)
    â†’ More accurate: excludes failed orders

11. ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) = Total Revenue / Total Ad Spend
    â†’ â‚¹5 ROAS = For every â‚¹1 spent, got â‚¹5 back
    â†’ Target: >3.0x minimum

----------------

    â†’ The actual profit after ALL costs

13. Profit Per Order = Net Profit / Delivered Orders

14. Platform Efficiency Score = (ROAS Ã— (1 - RTO%))
